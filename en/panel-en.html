EArte ALyC Overview Data

Based on master's and doctoral theses available in the databases of the countries participating in EArte ALyC, we present some general data, such as the number of works already catalogued, their temporal distribution, the level of the graduate programme (master’s or doctoral), the educational contexts, and the type of funding of the university or institution, distinguishing between public and private. We emphasize that the data do not represent the total number of theses in each country, but only those productions that have already been catalogued, as this is an ongoing process of identification and analysis. The update date refers to the most recent date on which the countries’ data were added to the website.
