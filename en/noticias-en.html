EArte ALyC News

EArte ALyC News was created to share the research team’s progress with the entire community. New editions will be published semiannually, containing relevant information about the group’s activities, such as scientific publications, events, and other topics of interest. The newsletter is interactive, allowing users to navigate previous editions, view in full screen, and access links when provided on the screen.
