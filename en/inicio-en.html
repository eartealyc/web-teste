WELCOME!

We are a collective of educators, researchers, and individuals interested in research in Environmental Education. Our aim is to select and analyze master’s dissertations and doctoral theses related to environmental issues and educational processes in Latin America and the Caribbean. The objective of this work is to contribute to the systematic and theoretical consolidation of the field of Environmental Education research.
